## What We Collect

### When You Place an Order

If you purchase a product through our shop, your order is processed by **Shopify**. Shopify collects the information necessary to complete your transaction, including:

- Name
- Email address
- Billing and shipping address
- Payment details

We do not store your payment information directly. For details on how Shopify handles your data, see [Shopify's Privacy Policy](https://www.shopify.com/legal/privacy).

### When You Join Our Waitlist

Our waitlist is hosted by **Typeform**. When you sign up, the information you provide (such as your name and email) is collected and stored by Typeform. See [Typeform's Privacy Policy](https://www.typeform.com/privacy-policy/) for details.

### When You Contact Us

If you reach out via email, we receive the information you choose to include in your message.

## Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies. We do not use analytics or tracking scripts. We do not track your behavior on this site.

## Third-Party Services

Our website loads resources from the following external services to function properly:

- **Google Fonts** and **cdnfonts** — for loading typefaces. These services may log your IP address when fonts are requested. See [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).
- **FontAwesome** — for loading icons.
- **Shopify** — for processing purchases.
- **Typeform** — for our waitlist form.

We do not use any social media tracking pixels or embedded widgets.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except as described above (Shopify for orders, Typeform for waitlist signups).

## Data Retention

Order data is retained as required by Swiss law and for the purposes of fulfilling and supporting your purchase. Waitlist data is retained until you unsubscribe or request deletion.

## Your Rights

Under Swiss data protection law, you have the right to:

- Access the personal data we hold about you
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Object to the processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at [contact@rooflesslabs.com](mailto:contact@rooflesslabs.com).
